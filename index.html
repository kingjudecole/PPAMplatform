The Man & The Mandate

A Prophet Raised
For Such a Time

In 1989, a young man in Dansoman, Accra, Ghana received a divine mandate that would change the spiritual landscape of West Africa. Prophet Prince Manasseh Atsu founded Watered Garden Church Ministry International from a burning conviction — that God's people deserved authentic prophetic ministry, not religious performance.

"God did not call us to build a church. He called us to build a Kingdom community — one life at a time, one nation at a time."

Thirty-five years later, that mandate is bearing fruit across six nations on three continents. Known for prophetic accuracy and apostolic authority, Prophet Atsu has become one of West Africa's most respected voices — his ministry marked by documented signs, healings and transformations that cannot be explained apart from God.

Global Mandate

Six Nations.
One Mandate.

What began in one community in Accra in 1989 has become a multi-national movement. PPMA Ministries International now has active chapters in Ghana, Nigeria, United Kingdom, Canada, United States and across West Africa.

Every branch carries the same prophetic DNA — not a franchise, but a family. United by a shared mandate to transform communities through the authentic power of the Gospel.
