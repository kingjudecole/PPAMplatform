The Man & The Mandate

A Prophet Raised
For Such A Time

Prophet Prince Manasseh Atsu is the founder and presiding prophet of Watered Garden Church Ministry International — a ministry born from a divine mandate received in 1989. From a small gathering in Dansoman, Accra, his apostolic influence now spans six nations across Africa, Europe and North America.

"God did not call us to build a church. He called us to build a Kingdom community — one life at a time, one nation at a time."

Known for prophetic accuracy, apostolic depth and the consistent demonstration of signs and wonders, Prophet Atsu is one of West Africa's most respected prophetic voices — his ministry marked by a rare convergence of the Word and the Spirit.
